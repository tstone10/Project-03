Facts

Japanese Fictional Character

Doraemon is a fictional character in the Japanese manga and anime series of the same name. It was created by Fujiko Fujio, the pen name of writing team Hiroshi Fujimoto and Motoo Abiko. He is a male robotic cat that travels back in time from the 22nd century to aid a preteen boy named Noby. An "official" birth certificate for the character gives him a birth date of 3 September 2112 and lists his city of residency as Kawasaki, Kanagawa, the city where the manga was created. In 2008, Japan's Foreign Ministry appointed Doraemon the country's "anime ambassador".

Conceived by Hiroshi Fujimoto

When searching for ideas for a new manga, Hiroshi Fujimoto wished a machine existed that would come up with ideas for him. He tripped over his daughter's toy, hearing that cats were fighting in his neighborhood. The name "Doraemon" can be translated roughly to "stray" in English. Remarkably, the name "Doraemon" is written in a mixture of two Japanese scripts: Katakana and Hiragana. "Dora" is derived from "dora neko" (brazen or stray cat). It is a corruption of nora (stray).
